About Lee

Engineer by trade, dad by joy, tinkerer by compulsion.

Hi there! I'm Lee McKinnon, a software developer and technical leader with over 20 years of experience building products and leading teams. I'm passionate about creating elegant solutions to complex problems and helping others grow in their technical careers.

Currently, I work as a technical leader where I focus on architecture, mentoring, and building high-performing engineering teams. I've worked across various technologies and industries, from startups to enterprise organizations.

What I Do

  • Lead engineering teams and drive technical strategy
  • Design and implement scalable software architectures
  • Mentor developers and help build technical career paths
  • Write about technology, leadership, and software development

Beyond Code

When I'm not coding or leading teams, I'm a devoted father enjoying the adventure of raising children in our digital age. I believe in the importance of work-life balance and often write about the challenges and joys of being both a technical leader and a present parent.

Let's Connect

I love connecting with other developers, technical leaders, and anyone interested in the intersection of technology and life. Feel free to reach out!
